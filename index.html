Wheel Spinner — Free Random Decision Maker

Can't decide what to eat, who goes first, or what to do? Let the Wheel Spinner decide for you! This free online tool lets you enter custom options and spin a colorful wheel to get a random result. No download, no signup — just instant fun.

How to Use

  • Type your options in the text box — one per line.
  • Click the SPIN button in the center of the wheel.
  • Wait for the wheel to stop and reveal your random winner!
  • Use preset buttons for quick starts (food, yes/no, numbers, etc.)

Popular Use Cases

  • What to eat — Can't decide dinner? Add your options and spin!
  • Random name picker — Fair way to choose who goes first in a game or task.
  • Yes or No — Let the wheel make tough decisions for you.
  • Classroom activities — Teachers use it to pick students or topics randomly.
  • Prize giveaways — Add participant names and spin to pick a winner.
  • Challenge roulette — Add dares or tasks and spin to see what you get.

Features

  • Custom options — Add up to 50 items, one per line.
  • Quick presets — Food, Yes/No, Numbers, Tasks, Names, Colors.
  • Shuffle — Randomize the order of your options before spinning.
  • Colorful wheel — Each section gets a vibrant color automatically.
  • Smooth animation — Realistic spinning with natural deceleration.
  • Mobile friendly — Works perfectly on phones and tablets.
  • 100% free — No limits, no signup, no download.

Is the Wheel Truly Random?

Yes! The wheel uses JavaScript's random number generator to determine the final stopping position. Each spin produces a completely unpredictable result, making it fair for all uses — from casual fun to classroom picks and giveaways.
