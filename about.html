About Wheel Spinner

Wheel Spinner is a free online tool that helps you make random decisions with a fun spinning wheel. Enter your options, spin, and let the wheel decide. No downloads, no signups — just instant fun.

How It Works

Type your options into the text box (one per line), then click SPIN. The wheel rotates with a smooth animation and stops on a random winner. Results are determined by a random number generator, ensuring fairness.

Use Cases

  • Deciding what to eat for dinner
  • Picking a random name from a list
  • Choosing who goes first in a game
  • Classroom random student selection
  • Giveaway and raffle drawings
  • Yes or No decisions
